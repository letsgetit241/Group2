Our Menu

At Cultural Cuisines, we take pride in offering a menu that celebrates global diversity. Each dish is crafted with care, using authentic recipes and fresh ingredients to bring the flavors of the world to your plate. Explore our menu options below:

Mexican Cuisine

  • Tacos al Pastor - Marinated pork, grilled pineapple, cilantro, and onions served on a warm corn tortilla. The perfect balance of sweet and savory. | $3.50
  • Guacamole & Chips - Creamy avocado, lime, onions, and spices served with crisp tortilla chips. A crowd-pleaser every time. | $4.00
  • Quesadillas - Melted cheese and your choice of chicken or vegetables folded into a grilled tortilla. A comforting classic. | $5.00

  • Fried Rice - Stir-fried rice with egg, vegetables, and your choice of chicken, shrimp, or tofu. | $7.00

Middle Eastern Cuisine
